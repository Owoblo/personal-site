John Owolabi

John Owolabi runs Saturn Star Movers—and realized most moving companies operate like it's 1995. So he's building the software stack the industry should have had decades ago. His work spans real estate data, logistics automation, and tools that turn manual chaos into scalable systems. More

He focuses on systems, not noise, and prefers solving real problems over talking about them.

He writes to think. His blog is where he breaks down what he's learning—ideas, experiments, failures, and whatever problem he's currently obsessed with. Curiosity drives most of his work — whether it's AI, markets, travel, design, or understanding how people operate.

He grew up learning to figure things out quickly, and that habit never left. He moves fast, asks direct questions, and takes work seriously. He likes meeting people, but he also keeps his distance. The mix is intentional.
